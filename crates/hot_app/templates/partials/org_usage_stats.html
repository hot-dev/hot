About Usage Limits:

  • Runs are counted per billing period and reset at the start of each cycle.
  • File Storage includes all files uploaded through your Hot applications.
  • Team Members limit is enforced - you cannot add members beyond your plan limit.
  • Call Data is automatically archived after the retention period expires.
  • Store Data includes all data in ::hot::store maps, including embeddings.
  • Compute Unit Seconds (CUS) measure container resource usage. Each box size has a multiplier applied to wall-clock seconds. Usage over the included amount is billed as overage.
